The Rooms

Check out these cool rooms!

Standard rooms

All Standard rooms are stylish and offer a range of amenities. They are available as Single, Double, or Twin for 1, 2, 3, or 4 people.

  • Comfortable beds with white soft duvets
  • Air conditioning
  • Bathtub with shower
  • Hairdryer
  • In-room safe
  • Mini-fridge
  • Same day dry cleaning and laundry (weekdays only)
  • Free access to the fitness centre
  • Free luggage storage
Variants:
  • Single
  • Double
  • Twin
  • Triple
  • Quadruple
  • Superior twin

Business rooms

Business Rooms are available as Double or Twin for 1 or 2 people and offer guests additional benefits:

  • Seating area with leather armchairs or a sofa bed
  • Extra living space
  • Long wooden desk
  • Everything mentioned in Standard Rooms
Variants:
  • Double
  • Twin
  • Superior double
  • Superior twin
