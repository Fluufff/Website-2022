Residential ticket

The best way to enjoy the convention to its full potential is with a residential ticket!

Please note that all prices listed here are for ONE spot in a shared bedroom and ONE convention spot. Reserving an entire room is possible, but at double price.

Residential tickets include:

  • Con area access
  • Access to all events
  • Sleeping at the hotel
  • Breakfast
  • Daily room cleaning

Check-in on your first day is at 16:00. Check-out on the last day is at 12:00.

Info about the different room types can be found on the rooms page. This year we have single and two person bedrooms

You can add an extra day before and after the convention, called “early arrival” and “late departure” respectively.

Day ticket

Do you want to experience our fluffy event but have no need for accommodation? We have just the thing for you! We offer convention tickets for any given day, the full event, or weekend tickets.

Day tickets include:

  • Con area access
  • Access to all events of the chosen day(s)

Please note that day tickets allow you to stay from the first planned event, until the end of the last one.
